What is this?

This website serves as a portfolio of random knowledge.
Just some random things like: strategies for games, guides, and other things.
This is mainly intended for personal use, and use by friends. Feel free to use as you like, stranger.



Why?

I often make text files on my computer just to keep notes,
but sometimes, I forget to take those notes, and they often get very scattered.
So, I've decided on making a simple bookmarkable website that I can access in my browser.
It also helps if I need to share a file or other thing with my friend, and having this instead of a google drive file is much easier.



Why make it public?

Some projects I've made I would say are good enough to be considered releasable,
and I hate having to deal with limitations from websites like steam or itch.
So, having a fully customizable website was my chosen solution.
If I ever make a game that requires a payment, I will not release it here.
I will definetly post updates and other announcements here though.
